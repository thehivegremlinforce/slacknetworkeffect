# Slack Connect — The Network Effect

An interactive visualization of how Slack Connect creates compounding network effects across customers, partners, vendors, AI agents, and apps & data — all sharing one fabric of channels.

Move the **Network Scale** slider from Startup to Global and watch the graph expand. Toggle Slackbot to see what changes when Slack's native assistant joins every Connect channel. Switch scenarios to focus on a single deal room, the AI agent layer, or the data plane.

## What it shows

- **Customers, Partners, Vendors** — the human side of the network, connected through shared Slack Connect channels
- **AI Agents** — autonomous workers operating inside the same channels as the humans they support
- **Apps & Data** — the integrations that turn channels into systems of record
- **Slackbot** — Slack's always-on native assistant, governed by your Enterprise policies

The point: every node added doesn't just connect to one other thing — it multiplies the value of everything already on the network.
